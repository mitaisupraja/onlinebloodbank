About

Blood bank is a place where blood bag that is collected from blood donation events is stored
in one place. The term blood bank refers to a division of a hospital laboratory where
the storage of blood product occurs and where proper testing is performed to reduce the
risk of transfusion related events.
The development of Web-based ONLINE BLOOD BANK MANAGEMENT SYSTEM is proposed to provide a management
functional to the blood bank in order to handle the blood bag and to make entries of the individuals who want to donate blood and who are in need.
